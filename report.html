About Marine Debris Tracker

The Marine Debris Tracker is a community-driven initiative aimed at identifying and reporting marine debris to promote a cleaner marine environment. By reporting debris, you contribute to data collection that helps government agencies and organizations take action against pollution in our oceans.

Your participation is crucial in raising awareness and fostering a cleaner, healthier marine ecosystem for future generations.
